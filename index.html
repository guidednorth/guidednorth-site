About Guided North

Digital expertise,
mission-first mindset

Craig Paulnock
Founder, Guided North Consulting

Guided North Consulting was founded on a simple belief: good organizations deserve the same quality of digital thinking as the world's largest companies, without the enterprise price tag or the consultant-speak.

I bring 20+ years of senior digital product, engineering, and platform leadership across for-profit and mission-driven organizations. From building Target's first mobile commerce experience to leading the Open Y platform adopted by 70+ YMCAs across North America, my work has consistently bridged rigorous digital execution with meaningful impact.

Guided North exists to bring that experience directly to the organizations doing the most important work, on a scale and budget that fits.
